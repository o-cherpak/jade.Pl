Questions

Bus tickets online

You can buy bus tickets online quickly and easily through our website. Just choose your departure and arrival cities, select the travel date, and pick a suitable time. Secure online payment options are available, and your e-ticket will be sent directly to your email or mobile.

Can I print my bus ticket

Yes, you can print your ticket. After purchasing online, you’ll receive a confirmation email with a printable version of your ticket. Simply download and print it before your trip.

What is an electronic ticket

An electronic ticket (e-ticket) is a digital version of your bus ticket. You receive it via email or app, and can show it on your phone when boarding—no need to print.

How can I return my bus tickets

You can return your tickets easily through our website or mobile app before the scheduled departure time. Please log in to your account, go to your bookings, and select the ticket you want to return. Follow the instructions to complete the return process. Note that refund policies may vary depending on the ticket type and the time remaining before departure. Some tickets might have partial refunds or fees for returns. For more details, check the refund policy on our site or contact customer support.
